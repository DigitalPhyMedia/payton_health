Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common patient questions

We offer a free initial consultation. Our fees are typically based on a percentage of the reimbursement we secure for you, meaning you only pay when we successfully recover your money. Contact us for specific details about your situation.

The timeline varies depending on your insurance provider and the complexity of your claim. Typically, claims are processed within 15-45 days. We expedite the process and keep you updated throughout.

You'll need medical bills, receipts, insurance policy details, discharge summaries, and any medical reports. Don't worry—we'll guide you through exactly what's needed for your specific case.

We specialize in handling denied claims. Our team will review the denial reason, prepare a comprehensive appeal, and represent you throughout the appeals process to maximize your chances of approval.
