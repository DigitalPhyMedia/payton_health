Frequently Asked Questions

Typically, you'll need original bills, receipts, discharge summary, and medical reports. A claim form from the insurer is also required.

Claim processing times vary by insurer but generally range from 15 to 30 days for reimbursement claims. Cashless claims are usually approved within hours.

If your claim is rejected, first understand the reason for rejection from the insurer. You can then rectify the issue (e.g., provide missing documents) and re-submit the claim.
