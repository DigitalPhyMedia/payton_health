Nature of Services

  1. Connecting Patients and Hospitals: It allows patients to identify and connect with hospitals offering financial assistance options through Payton. It helps hospitals to manage and process Insurance claims applications effectively through us.
  2. Application Facilitation: Provides a structured framework for patients to submit their claim applications, including necessary documentation. Helps hospitals to review, track, and process applications in a timely manner, including the financial and other backgrounds of patients.
  3. Information Repository: Serves as an educational resource for patients, providing details about eligibility criteria, and claim processes. Helps hospitals to communicate options and requirements to patients.
  4. Transparency and Status Tracking: Enables patients to track the status of their claim applications in real-time. Facilitates clear and transparent communication between patients and hospitals regarding financial aid decisions.
  5. Support and Guidance: Offers support to patients in understanding their options and completing the claim application process. Assists hospitals with managing claim applications and required documents and addressing patient queries.
  6. eNACH Facility: This Platform provides services to facilitate the setup of eNACHmandates for recurring or one-time payments. Act as a conduit for sharing mandate- related information between patients, banks, and hospitals. Offer users tracking andstatus updates for their eNACH mandates.

The platform does not provide direct financial assistance or funding, make decisions regarding the approval or denial of processing of claims, or offer medical advice, treatment, or healthcare services.

Disclaimer: The platform is not responsible for the final decision or outcome of processing of claim documents and acts solely as a facilitator to enhance the efficiency of the process.
