1. Refund Overview

Our Refund Policy outlines the terms for refunds related to our services. We aim for transparency and fairness in all our financial dealings. Eligibility for a refund depends on the service type and the terms outlined in your specific service agreement.

2. Eligibility for a Refund

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. You may be eligible if services were not rendered as described in the service agreement or in case of a billing error on our part. Fees for services already successfully completed (e.g., a successfully processed claim) are non-refundable.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at billing@paytonhealthcare.com within 30 days of the charge. Your request must include your name, contact information, service details, and a clear reason for the request.

4. Processing

Once we receive your request, our team will review it. We may contact you for additional information. You will be notified of the approval or rejection of your refund within 10-15 business days. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment.
