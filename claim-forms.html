Common Form Mistakes to Avoid

Learn from common errors to ensure smooth claim processing

close
Incomplete Information

Leaving fields blank or providing partial information can delay or reject your claim. Always complete every required field.

close
Missing Signatures

Forgetting to sign or date the form is one of the most common reasons for claim rejection. Review before submission.

close
Wrong Form Selection

Using the wrong form for your claim type can cause delays. Ensure you download the correct form for your specific situation.

close
Missing Attachments

Submitting forms without required supporting documents will result in rejection. Use our checklist to verify all documents are included.
